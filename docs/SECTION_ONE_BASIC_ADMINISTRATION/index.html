SECTION ONE: BASIC ADMINISTRATION

Welcome to the digital trenches. Basic Administration is the survival gear you need before you go poking around production servers. We're covering the absolute essentials: how to turn things on (and off again), wielding root powers without accidentally nuking your career, and figuring out what all those rogue processes are actually doing.

We'll also dive into user management (because humans are inherently the weakest link), taming the sprawling wilderness of the filesystem, and backups—literally the only thing standing between you and a very awkward conversation with your boss. Grab a coffee. It's time to learn how to keep the lights on.

Chapter 1: Where to Start

Welcome to the jungle. We’ve designed this guide to cut through the massive, overwhelming ecosystem of archaic man pages, dead blogs, and dusty reference manuals that sysadmins usually have to suffer through.

The Orientation Guide

Think of this as your survival map. We review the major systems, identify the moving parts, and show you exactly how they connect. When there are ten ways to do the same thing, we'll tell you which one actually works and which ones will get you paged at 3 AM.

The Quick-Reference Handbook

You don't need to memorize the 80 different flags for the ps command. We boil everything down to the cheat codes you actually need to do your job efficiently without wasting brain space.

Serious, Professional Administration

Setting up a single server is easy. Keeping a massive, distributed platform alive during a viral traffic spike while dodging targeted attacks? That takes skill. We’re giving you the techniques and rules of thumb to scale your empire and recover gracefully when everything catches fire.

Are We Objective?

Absolutely not. Sysadmins will fight to the death over the "right" way to do things. We're giving you our battle-tested, highly opinionated takes. Take what works for you, and ignore the rest.
